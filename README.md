It started with a simple question: *What if a Product Manager didn't just write tickets, but actually built the vision?* For years, I lived in the world of PRDs, roadmaps, and "coming soon" stickers. 
But when the AI revolution hit, the wall between "thinking" and "building" collapsed. I realized that with the right AI orchestration, a product vision could become a working prototype in hours rather than months. My journey into AI building began with a quest to automate the tedious parts of product management and quickly evolved into a passion for creating agentic workflows and AI-native applications. Today, I don't just manage the "what" and the "why"—I build the "how."

---

### 🤖 My AI Arsenal
I am currently deep in the ecosystem of **generative AI** and **agentic workflows**. My daily toolkit revolves around **Cursor** , **Calude Code**, **Antigravity** for AI-native development and **Opus**, **Gemini** as my primary reasoning engine for complex logic. I’ve integrated **LangChain** and **LangGraph** for building stateful, multi-agent systems that can handle real-world ambiguity. On the learning front, I am currently mastering **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** architectures using **Pinecone**, exploring **fine-tuning techniques for Small Language Models (SLMs)**.
